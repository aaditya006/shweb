About Us

Shreya Hearing Aids Clinic, located in Bangalore, is a premier hub for comprehensive hearing solutions. Our skilled audiologists provide personalized care, using state-of-the-art diagnostics to tailor interventions. From precise evaluations to top-tier hearing aid fittings and repairs, our services cover a wide spectrum. We offer not only advanced technology but also support, resources, and communication strategies, fostering an environment where patients and families can navigate their hearing journey confidently. With expertise and empathy, Shreya Hearing Aids Clinic is dedicated to improving lives through exceptional hearing care.

Profile Of Dr.Sathya Ranna

A dream run towards service and success!

Dr. Sathya Ranna is a pioneer in the field of private Hearing Aid dispensing associated with rehabilitation services. She is an award winning entrepreneur.

Almost twelve years ago in 1998 she made a humble beginning with a solitary unit of Shreya Hearing Clinic at Indiranagar. In the years to follow due to her sustained efforts and passion towards the cause, to day she has expanded into two states with series of multi city units, fully equipped with state of art infrastructure and offering sophisticated services.

Inspired to the core towards the subject of Speech & Hearing, later she completed her graduation and master degree as a gold medalist. Simultaneously she has achieved this feet as UGC scholar under Mysore University. Further she went on to add one more feather to her cap, as she earned her doctorate from the prominent institution like All India Institute of Speech & Hearing under University of Mysore.

As a successful entrepreneur, she has been recognised by various awards. Significant among them are, Siemens Annual Excellence Award (which she is winning consistently for many years) and Best Entrepreneur award in Audiology from Indian Speech & Hearing Association. In the scientific and intellectual world, she is known with her articles to medical Journals and through her participation in various National level conferences, where she has presented her research work.
